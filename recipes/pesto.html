Pesto

Description

A homemade pesto from 4 simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • A bundle of Basil
  • Half a cup of Pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Soak Basil with water and soap to get rid of bugs, dirt and pesticides
  2. Wash thoroughly
  3. Optional: soak the pine nuts for a while to get the maximum nutritional value
  4. Put all ingredients together in the blender and mix(/blend?)
  5. Separate to jars and freeze what you are not using. Lasts for a couple of days in the fridge, a couple of months in the freezer
