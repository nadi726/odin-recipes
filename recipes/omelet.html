Omelet

Description

A simple omelet

Ingredients

  • 1-2 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: Coriander and/or parsley, chopped into small pieces

Steps

  1. Break the eggs and beat them with salt
  2. If you decided to add Coriander/parsley, add them now
  3. Put a pan on the stove with a bit of olive oil and light the stove to a medium-small fire
  4. After the oil has heated, put your beaten eggs on the pan
  5. Once the lower side of the omelet gets cooked, flip it.
  6. When the omelet is done, move it to a plate
