Tahini

Description

A homemade tahini made from raw tahini. Easy to make and delicious, I combine it with a large variety of dishes.

Ingredients

  • Raw tahini
  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the raw tahini with water, adding water periodically until it is as smooth as you want it to be
  2. Add lemon and salt according to taste
